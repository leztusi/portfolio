About

Cognitio Terra as a math-themed character action game where you chain flashy combos and combine them with math attacks. You play as Terra, An ARCSEC agent, protecting the Oasis City, and the whole world from the NMEs, an interdimensional entities that declared the war against the humanity.

Development

I'm the creator of Cognitio Terra, originally made as my college capstone project and was later awarded as the best capstone project in 2016. I later started to rebuild the project around 2017 with the goal of achieving better gameplay and visuals. Today, the development team consists of 3 members including me who works on the character design and story, and another on sounds and music. I was involved in almost all aspects of the development (programming, 3D art and animation, level design, VFX and shaders, cinematics, and sound design).

Cognitio Terra is Made with Unity and still under development.

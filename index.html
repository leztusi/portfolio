Project 1 : Cognitio Terra, Extreme Mathematical Action

❮ ❯

Cognitio Terra is a fast paced video game mixed with math games originally made by Bob Lester Tusi for his final IT Capstone (Thesis). Despite the experimental gameplay, The Thesis version was preached and recieved good feedback from the panelists and the project was awarded with Best IT Capstone project in 2016. The project was later continued with Disaster Squad Games, a small indie game studio consists 5 members in which Bob is the Lead Developer who mainly works on gameplay programming, animations, lighting and special effects.

Gameplay

You play as Terra, who uses CORE to Assist her and "calculate" her actions on critical scenarios, giving her great reflexes and more acurate attacks.



Cognitio Terra is Made with Unity and still under development.
